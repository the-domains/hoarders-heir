Hoarder's Heir ♕ A Prop Duo

Partners, Kenneth Hyatt & Justin Schram, have been working as freelance Prop Stylist since 2010. Hyatt, a College of Charleston alumni, and Schram, graduate of the Charleston School of the Arts, have backgrounds in Piano, Creative Writing and Studio Arts. Having a keen eye for design and composition, the stylist duo have an innate way of creating that is unique to their field. Working on projects in fashion, food and beyond, their client list includes the likes of LL Bean, Le Creuset, Johnston & Murphy and Jack Daniels. In addition, their work has been featured in Nation-wide and International publications like Garden & Gun, Food & Wine, Better Homes & Gardens and The New York Times Magazine.
